# Billing & Payments

Manage your billing, invoices, and payment methods.

## Payment Methods

OTH accepts the following payment methods:

- **Credit/Debit Cards** - Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- **PayPal** - Link your PayPal account
- **Crypto** - Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT
- **Account Balance** - Pre-load funds for automatic payments

## Adding a Payment Method

1. Go to **Account** > **Billing**
2. Click **Add Payment Method**
3. Choose your preferred method
4. Enter your details and save

## Understanding Invoices

Each invoice includes:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Invoice # | Unique identifier |
| Due Date | Payment deadline |
| Services | List of billed services |
| Total | Amount due |
| Status | Paid, Pending, or Overdue |

## Auto-Pay

Enable auto-pay to never miss a payment:

1. Go to **Billing** > **Settings**
2. Toggle **Auto-Pay** on
3. Select your default payment method

> **Note**: You'll receive an email 3 days before any automatic charge.

## Refund Policy

- **Within 24 hours**: Full refund, no questions asked
- **Within 7 days**: Prorated refund available
- **After 7 days**: Contact support for special cases
