# Getting Started

Welcome to OTH! This guide will help you get up and running quickly.

## Creating an Account

1. Visit [oth.com](https://oth.com) and click **Sign Up**
2. Enter your email address and create a password
3. Verify your email address
4. Complete your profile setup

## Your Dashboard

Once logged in, you'll see your dashboard with:

- **Overview** - Quick stats and recent activity
- **Services** - Your active hosting services
- **Billing** - Invoices and payment methods
- **Support** - Access to help resources
